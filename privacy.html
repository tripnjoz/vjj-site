1. Introduction

Vegas Jackpot Junkies LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates VegasJackpotJunkies.com and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By using Vegas Jackpot Junkies you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Username — displayed publicly on your submissions if not anonymous
  • Password — stored securely and encrypted, never visible to us
  • Progressive jackpot submissions — amounts, game type, venue, timestamp
  • Photo submissions — photos of machine displays for verification purposes
  • Venue information — if you claim or manage a venue on our platform

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and browser information
  • IP address — used for security and fraud prevention
  • GPS location data — only when you submit a progressive amount and only at time of submission
  • Usage data — which screens you visit, how you interact with the app
  • Submission timestamps — when amounts were submitted

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Display progressive jackpot information to other users
  • Calculate and display your trust score and submission accuracy
  • Send account related notifications you have opted into
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or inaccurate submissions
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account or our service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated decision making that significantly affects you, and we do not build advertising profiles based on your activity.

4. Information We Never Sell

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Your email address and personal details are used solely to operate and improve the Vegas Jackpot Junkies service.

5. GPS and Location Data

When you submit a progressive jackpot amount, we may collect GPS coordinates at the time of submission. This location data is used exclusively to:

  • Verify that submissions are made from the location of the venue
  • Attach geographic context to submissions for data integrity purposes
  • Display as part of the verified submission badge

We do not track your location continuously. Location is only captured at the moment of a voluntary submission. You may submit anonymously, in which case your username is not displayed publicly, however location data may still be used for internal verification purposes.

6. Photo Submissions

When you submit a photo of a machine display for verification purposes:

  • Photos are stored securely on our servers
  • Photos are used only to verify the accuracy of submitted progressive amounts
  • Photos are reviewed by our moderation team and may be reviewed by venue staff
  • Photos are not shared publicly or used for any purpose other than verification
  • You retain ownership of photos you submit but grant us a license to use them for verification purposes

7. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Public submissions — if you submit a progressive amount without selecting anonymous, your username may be displayed alongside your submission

  • Service providers — we use Supabase for database and authentication services. They process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. We use industry standard measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission
  • Secure password hashing — your password is never stored in plain text
  • Row level security on our database — users can only access their own data
  • Regular security reviews of our platform

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our service. Specifically:

  • Account information is retained until you request deletion
  • Submission history may be retained in anonymized form even after account deletion to maintain the integrity of our historical data
